# Re-Smeltables
Minecraft mod to re-smelt ingots into raw ore for purpose of XP farms!

# Update plans:
I eventually want to add 2 new types of firecharges one thats extra hot, and the other thats fully put out, and add a recipe for all three kinds aswell as just using it as a new item for re-smelting, the idea is that you take the firecharges smelt them to get them extra hot for XP, and then put them out using water or time!

Also I was originally thinking about making a re-smeltable powder item but I thought of the ingots and that was a much better idea in my opinion, although I'm thinking about having a step between where the it gives burning hot raw ore that needs to also be cooled like the firecharges im not sure yet, for now its simple and is just instant resmelting!
